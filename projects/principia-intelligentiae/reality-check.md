# Principia Intelligentiae — Reality Check
## Mendeleev + Maxwell + Hawking: Theory vs Known Deep Learning Phenomena

### Scorecard

| # | Phenomenon | Verdict | Explanation |
|---|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | **Neural scaling laws** | PARTIALLY EXPLAINS | Scale invariance (Symmetry #3) guarantees power-law form, but exponent α cannot yet be calculated from first principles. Shape yes, coefficients no. |
| 2 | **Double descent** | PARTIALLY EXPLAINS | Interpretable as coordinate singularity on Fisher manifold at interpolation threshold, with Λ providing implicit regularization beyond it. Needs explicit curvature divergence calculation. |
| 3 | **Grokking** | PARTIALLY EXPLAINS / PREDICTS NEW | False vacuum decay: memorization is metastable, generalization is true ground state. **New prediction**: time-to-grok scales exponentially with calculable curvature barrier height. |
| 4 | **Lottery ticket hypothesis** | PARTIALLY EXPLAINS | Permutation symmetry (S_n) explains degeneracy. Fails to explain why winning tickets exist at initialization before training breaks symmetry. |
| 5 | **Feature universality** | **EXPLAINS** | Strongest contact point. Unique low-curvature attractor on Fisher manifold implies convergent representations up to reparameterization. Natural geometric prediction. |
| 6 | **Emergence** | DOESN'T EXPLAIN | Framework is smooth/continuous; emergence is discrete/topological. Would require a theory of topological transitions on Fisher manifold — not yet in the framework. |

### Summary

- **1 full explanation** (feature universality)
- **4 partial explanations** (scaling laws, double descent, grokking, lottery tickets)
- **1 failure** (emergence — requires topological extensions)
- **1 novel prediction** (grokking as false vacuum decay with exponential time scaling)

### Hawking's Assessment

> "The theory is in the position of general relativity circa 1916 — the field equations are written down, a few exact solutions exist, but the zoo of phenomena (black holes, gravitational waves, cosmology) will take decades to derive. The question is whether someone runs the experiment this month."
